Solutions

Three solutions to prevent from killing animals sea life!

Don't throw away plastic

When plastic gets thrown in the sea/ocean, there are several consequences. Whether it be: sea animals suffocating from plastic, unknowingly switching diets from nutritional food to hardy plastics, or even falling victim to the murderous plan of a piece of debris, it all leads to death.

Re-use plastic bags

If you see your relatives or close friends throwing away plastic bags, tell them not to. Make them realize that by throwing a single straw down the drains it's desnination is in a sea life stomach.

Help Clean Up the Beaches

Gather some friends and tell them that you're going to go pick up plastic and any trash that doesn't belong to be on the beach. Tell them it's for a good cause, otherwise we're going to have beaches filled with plastic, they should know that plastic on the beach means no more late afternoons out on the beach.
